What is OILSOIL

How To Contribute

We are committed to caring for the environment and promoting sustainable practices in the automotive industry.

One of the major concerns related to vehicle maintenance is the impact of oil changes on the environment. Every year, millions of liters of used oil are drained from vehicles during oil changes, and improper disposal of this oil can cause soil and water contamination, which can harm the environment and human health. In fact, a typical oil change involves draining and replacing approximately 4 to 5 liters (or about 1.3 gallons) of oil from a vehicle's engine. If this oil is not disposed of properly, it can contaminate soil and groundwater with toxic chemicals, such as benzene, lead, and arsenic, which can have harmful effects on human health and the environment. Some types of oil can take many years or even decades to fully decompose.

In addition, "Every car wash wastes an average of 70 liters of water". We promote the use of eco-friendly and efficient steam washing techniques that can reduce water usage by up to 90% compared to traditional washing methods.Steam washing is an effective and safe way to remove dirt, stains, and bacteria from your car without the use of harsh chemicals, making it a win-win situation for both your car and the environment.

By taking steps to prevent oil spills, using high-quality oil, and promoting eco-friendly car washing practices, we can make a positive impact on the environment and protect our health and the health of future generations. Join us in our effort to protect the environment and promote sustainable practices in the automotive industry. Remember, every small step counts towards a greener future!
